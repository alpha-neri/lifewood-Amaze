AI Scanning

Case Study: AI Data Extraction for Global Archives

The Challenge: The Global Heritage Archives held millions of priceless, fragile historical documents. Manual digitization and transcription were projected to take decades, with significant risk of data entry errors and document damage.

Our Solution: Lifewood engineered a comprehensive AI-powered pipeline. We deployed high-speed, non-contact scanners integrated with our custom computer vision models to auto-classify documents, correct for page curl, and enhance text. Our advanced Optical Character Recognition (OCR) engine, trained on 18th and 19th-century cursive and print, transcribed the content with 99.4% accuracy. The extracted text and metadata were then structured into a searchable digital database.

Client: Global Heritage Archives
Timeline: 24 Months
Result: Reduced project timeline by 90% and created a fully indexed, searchable digital archive.

Case Study: AI-Powered Record Linkage for LegacyFinds

The Challenge: LegacyFinds, a leading genealogy platform, needed to connect individuals across millions of disparate records (census data, ship manifests, birth certificates). Variations in names, dates, and locations made manual connections nearly impossible at scale.

Our Solution: Lifewood designed a sophisticated entity resolution model using a combination of NLP and graph database technologies. The AI learns to recognize name variations (e.g., "William" vs. "Bill"), infers family relationships from context, and calculates a probability score for each potential match between records. This automated "record linkage" engine works tirelessly in the background, surfacing new ancestral discoveries for users.

Client: LegacyFinds.com
Timeline: 18 Months (Phase 1)
Result: Generated over 50 million new, high-confidence ancestral connections for users in the first six months post-launch.

Case Study: NLP for Financial Market Intelligence

The Challenge: A major investment firm, Finacore Capital, was overwhelmed by the sheer volume of unstructured text data from news feeds, analyst reports, and SEC filings. Their analysts spent more time searching for information than analyzing it.

Our Solution: We built a custom NLP pipeline to ingest and analyze financial documents in real-time. The system uses Named Entity Recognition (NER) to tag companies and key personnel, and a fine-tuned sentiment analysis model to gauge market perception. All insights are fed into a live dashboard, allowing analysts to immediately spot market-moving events, track competitor sentiment, and identify hidden risks and opportunities.

Client: Finacore Capital
Timeline: 10 Months
Result: Increased analyst efficiency by over 300% and provided data for several high-performing trading strategies.

Case Study: AI Chatbot for a Leading Telco

The Challenge: A national telecommunications provider was experiencing record-high call volumes, leading to 20+ minute customer wait times and plummeting CSAT scores. Operational costs for their call center were spiraling out of control.

Case Study: Computer Vision for Manufacturing Quality Control

The Challenge: Precision Circuits Ltd. manufactures complex microchips where even microscopic defects can lead to catastrophic failure. Their manual inspection process was slow, expensive, and unable to detect all critical flaws, resulting in a high defect rate.

Our Solution: Lifewood implemented a real-time computer vision system on the assembly line. High-resolution cameras capture images of each microchip, which are then analyzed by our custom-trained anomaly detection model. The AI can identify defects invisible to the human eye, such as micro-cracks and soldering imperfections, with 99.98% accuracy. Any flagged chip is automatically removed from the line.

Client: Precision Circuits Ltd.
Timeline: 12 Months
Result: Reduced the final product defect rate by 97%, increased production throughput by 40%, and saved millions in warranty claims.
